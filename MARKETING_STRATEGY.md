# Jisr-Pay 🌉 — Global Marketing & Growth Strategy

> **"Remittances at the Speed of Stellar: 0.4% Fees. 5-Second Settlement."**
> The blueprint to turn [Jisr-Pay](https://jisr-pay.vercel.app/) into a globally recognized cross-border fintech giant across Gulf ↔ Africa corridors and beyond.

---

## 🎯 Executive Summary & Market Opportunity

The **Gulf ↔ Africa remittance corridor** handles over **$45 Billion annually**. Traditional money transmitters (Western Union, bank wires) extract up to **6.5% + $15 per transfer** and take 2–3 business days.

**Jisr-Pay disruptor advantage:**
- **0.4% Flat Fee** (up to **90% savings** over banks)
- **~5-Second Settlement** on the Stellar blockchain
- **3 Autonomous AI Agents** (Rate-Scout, Router, Reconciler) optimizing every route
- **Dual English/Arabic Native UX** built for Gulf & African users

| Persona | Demographics & Context | Core Pain Point | Jisr-Pay Solution |
|---|---|---|---|

## 🚀 4-Phase Go-To-Market (GTM) Roadmap

```
Phase 1: Hackathon & Web3 Validation (Months 1–3)
  └── Win Stellar Hackathon ➔ Secure Foundation Grants ➔ Viral X/Telegram Launch

Phase 2: Gulf ↔ Africa Diaspora Growth (Months 3–6)
  └── Localized TikTok/IG Campaigns ➔ Dubai/Lagos Community Ambassadors ➔ Referral Loop

Phase 3: B2B & Fiat Anchor Expansion (Months 6–12)
  └── Partner with M-Pesa / Yellow Card / Flutterwave ➔ Merchant API Release

Phase 4: Global Brand Dominance (Year 2+)
  └── Global PR (Forbes, CoinDesk, TechCrunch) ➔ Expansion into LatAm & SE Asia
```

---

### **Phase 1: Web3 Launch & Foundation Support (Months 1–3)**
* **Goal**: Establish technical credibility, secure grants, and build the early core community.
1. **Stellar Community Fund (SCF) Grant Application**:
   - Apply for Stellar Foundation grants ($50k–$150k funding) for liquidity bootstrapping.
2. **Web3 Tech PR**:
   - Pitch Jisr-Pay to *CoinDesk*, *Decrypt*, *Bankless*, and *Stellar Ecosystem Digest* highlighting the 3-Agent AI architecture.
3. **Developer & Hackathon Showcase**:
   - Open-source core SDKs and publish developer documentation for Soroban smart contract integration.

---

### **Phase 2: Gulf ↔ Africa Diaspora Acquisition (Months 3–6)**
* **Goal**: Drive retail adoption among expat communities in Dubai, Riyadh, Lagos, and Nairobi.
1. **The "Fee Challenge" Social Campaign (TikTok / Instagram / YouTube Shorts)**:
   - Short video format: *"I sent $500 home with my bank vs. with Jisr-Pay"*. Show the live receipt comparison ($35 saved!).
2. **Diaspora Ambassador Program**:
   - Recruit community leaders, university student associations, and expat workers in Dubai/Riyadh to serve as paid Jisr Ambassadors.
3. **Viral Referral Growth Engine**:
   - *"Give $10, Get $10"*: Every user who invites a friend gets $10 in fee credits after their friend's first transfer.

---

### **Phase 3: B2B Merchant & Anchor Partnerships (Months 6–12)**
* **Goal**: Expand liquidity and integrate directly into local payout rails.
1. **Stellar Anchor Integration**:
   - Integrate with established African & Gulf Stellar anchors (Yellow Card, ClickPesa, Fonbnk) for seamless local currency cash-out to M-Pesa, MTN Mobile Money, and local banks.
2. **Jisr Merchant API**:
   - Launch an API for SME merchants to invoice and collect cross-border payments directly on Stellar with 0.4% fees.

---

### **Phase 4: Global Brand Dominance (Year 2+)**
* **Goal**: Scale into a household name for global remittances.
1. **Global Press & Media Push**:
   - Featured stories on *TechCrunch*, *Forbes Middle East*, *Bloomberg Africa*, and *Financial Times*.
2. **Expansion to New High-Volume Corridors**:
   - Expand from Gulf ↔ Africa into **GCC ↔ South Asia** (AED ➔ INR/PKR) and **Europe ➔ LatAm** (EUR ➔ BRL/COP).
